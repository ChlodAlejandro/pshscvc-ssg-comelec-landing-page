Special thanks is given to all the Commission on Election Commissioners who helped make this happen, and to our Supreme Student Government adviser, Mr. Lyndon Lazaro.

We'll meet again for the induction and oath-taking of incoming SSG officers, to be conducted on October 25, 2021.
